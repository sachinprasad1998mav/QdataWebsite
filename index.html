One of the fastest growing IT Staffing Agencies in the U.S.

Whether you’re looking to find your next IT career, add qualified talent to meet your IT staffing needs, or join a highly energized and passionate team of staffing professionals, we can help you achieve your goal.

Why DIVERSANT?

DIVERSANT is a certified minority business enterprise and is ranked as one of the largest IT staffing firms in the U.S. The quality of our services is uncompromised, per our Net Promoter Score of 73. Our firm utilizes a third-party platform to administer surveys and calculate results for our clients and candidates. Our service offerings include Temporary Staffing, Contract-to-Hire, Direct Hire, and Managed Solutions.
