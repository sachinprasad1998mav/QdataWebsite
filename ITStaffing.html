Staff Augmentation

At Qdata, we handle a wide range of projects and tasks tailored to our clients' needs. It's well recognized that no organization can keep the best talent on payroll for every possible requirement. Therefore, contingency measures, such as providing temporary services, increasing capacity, making ad hoc appointments to fulfill current obligations, sourcing specialized skills, and covering for absences, vacations, or illnesses, have become standard practice. Staff augmentation is a key strategy to address these needs. QData has been supporting both startups and established organizations in leveraging staff augmentation, consistently satisfying a diverse client base for many years.

Recruiment Process Outsourcing

At QData, we recognize that recruitment processes in organizations aiming to attract top talent must be well-defined and aligned with company culture and goals. Both recruiting staff and managers need to be fully aware of the necessary steps to meet the organization's needs and objectives. In these times of rapid, multi-dimensional technological changes, efficient recruitment processes are essential. We have developed a flexible, hierarchical toolkit for staff and managers to follow, ensuring that your organization's mission and vision are seamlessly integrated.
Our regularly updated questionnaires, along with technical and soft-skill requirements, streamline the process for prospective candidates to apply. We also understand the importance of training recruiting staff and managers, even though it may seem redundant and boring. This training, often overlooked, is crucial for maintaining quality and speed. We offer to guide your recruiting team, providing valuable insights and actionable feedback to make the process result-oriented and efficient, requiring minimal external support. Our training covers all facets of recruitment practices, including data collection, updating, analysis, latest trends, future insights, interview questionnaire banks, and onboarding details.
